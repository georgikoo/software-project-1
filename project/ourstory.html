Our Story

Gacias Tavern was founded in 1990 by John and Maria Gacias. They immigrated to the United States from Greece in the 1970s and worked in various restaurants before deciding to open their own. They wanted to share the traditional Greek recipes they had grown up with and create a warm, welcoming atmosphere for their customers.

The restaurant quickly became popular among the local community and gained a reputation for its delicious food and friendly service. Over the years, John and Maria's children joined the family business and helped to expand the menu and renovate the interior.

Today, Gacias Tavern continues to be a family-owned and operated restaurant, serving authentic Greek cuisine in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. We are proud to carry on the traditions that John and Maria started over 30 years ago.
